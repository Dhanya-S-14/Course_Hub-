TCS DSA Patterns

Focus on basic logic - NOT like product companies!

MUST PREPARE

  • Arrays: Sum, Max/Min, Frequency count, Searching
  • Strings: Palindrome, Reverse, Count characters
  • Basic Math: Factorial, Prime number, Fibonacci
  • Sorting: Bubble sort, Selection sort
  • Recursion: Factorial, Fibonacci
  • Patterns: Star patterns, Number patterns

NOT REQUIRED

  • Dynamic Programming
  • Advanced Graphs
  • Hard LeetCode Problems
  • Complex Data Structures
  • Tries & Segment Trees
  • Advanced Algorithms

REAL INSIGHT from candidates:

"Focus on arrays and strings... basic coding is enough for TCS. Don't waste time on DP and hard LeetCode!"

Quick Tips for Success

Expert advice from successful candidates

Aptitude First (60% Time)

Focus most of your preparation time on aptitude - it's the elimination round. If you fail aptitude, you won't proceed to interviews.

Basic DSA is Enough

Arrays & Strings are enough for the coding round. Don't waste time on Dynamic Programming or Hard LeetCode problems.

Communication is Key

For service companies, communication skills are VERY IMPORTANT. Practice speaking clearly and confidently.

Practice, Practice, Practice

Solve at least 50 aptitude questions and 30 coding problems before your actual exam. Quality matters more than quantity.

Time Management

In the NQT exam, manage your time wisely. Don't get stuck on difficult questions - move on and come back if time permits.
