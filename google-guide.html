Google Courses Enrollment Guide

Step-by-step guide to enroll in Google certificates on Coursera

1

Create an Account

  • Go to Coursera website
  • Click Sign Up
  • Use your Gmail (recommended) OR any email
  • Verify your email address
2

Search for Google Courses

In the search bar, type:

  • "Google Data Analytics"
  • "Google UX Design"
  • "Google IT Support"
  • "Google Cybersecurity"
  • "Google Project Management"
3

Select Your Course

Choose a course that interests you:

  • Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate
  • Google UX Design Certificate
  • Google IT Support Certificate
  • Google Cybersecurity Certificate
  • Google Project Management Certificate

Tip

Click on the course to open its details page and read about the curriculum.

4

Click "Enroll for Free"

On the course page, you'll see the button "Enroll for Free". Click it to proceed with enrollment.

5

Choose Your Learning Option

You'll get 2 options to choose from:

Free Option

(Audit Mode)

✓ Access course content

✗ No certificate

Paid Option

(Certificate)

✓ Full access

✓ Certificate included

~₹3,000/month

Apply for Financial Aid (IMPORTANT!)

If you don't want to pay, you can get 100% FREE access!

  1. Click "Financial Aid Available" link
  2. Fill a simple form:
    • Why you need aid
    • Your learning goals
  3. Submit and wait ~15 days for approval
  4. Once approved → 100% FREE with certificate!

Success!

Financial aid approval gives you COMPLETE FREE access including the certificate!

7

Start Learning

  • Watch video lectures
  • Complete quizzes and assignments
  • Participate in discussion forums
  • Take practice tests
8

Get Your Certificate 🎉

After completing all modules:

  • Finish all required modules and assessments
  • Download your certificate
  • Add it to your Resume
  • Add it to your LinkedIn profile
  • Share it on social media!

Bonus Tip!

"You don't need money to learn skills — apply for financial aid and get Google certificates for mostly FREE!"
