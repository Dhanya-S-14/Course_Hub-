Free IBM Courses on Coursera

Financial Aid - Study FREE with Certificate

Available on Coursera

Steps to Apply:

  • Open IBM course on Coursera
  • Click "Financial Aid Available"
  • Fill application (basic questions)
  • Wait ~15 days for approval
  • Get 100% FREE access + certificate

Tips for Application:

  • Write why you need financial help
  • Mention your student status
  • Explain your career goals
  • Keep it genuine and concise
