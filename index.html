Skill India - How to Enroll

About Skill India

Skill India Mission is a Government of India initiative that provides 100% free skill development courses with certificates.

AI & Digital Skills Programming (C, Java, Python) Cloud Computing Data Entry & IT Communication Skills Healthcare, Retail
  • Government-recognized certificate
  • Free training (no cost)
  • Placement support in some courses

Eligibility

  • Indian citizen
  • Minimum: 10th / 12th pass (varies by course)
  • Basic English or Hindi knowledge
  • Valid mobile number & Aadhaar

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Mobile Number
  • Email ID
  • Passport-size photo
  • Educational details

How to Enroll (Step-by-Step)

Method 1: Online (Best for you)

Go to: skillindiadigital.gov.in
  1. Click "Register / Sign Up"
  2. Enter: Name
  3. Enter: Mobile number (OTP verification)
  4. Enter: Email & password
  5. Complete profile (education, skills)
  6. Browse courses
  7. Click "Enroll Now"
  8. Start learning instantly 🎓

Entire process takes only a few minutes!

Method 2: Training Center (Offline)

  • Visit Skill India website
  • Click "Find Training Center"
  • Choose nearby center
  • Register with documents
  • Attend training physically

After Enrollment

  • Access courses in "My Courses" dashboard
  • Watch videos + complete quizzes
  • Track progress
  • Download certificate after completion 📄
