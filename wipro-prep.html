Wipro DSA Patterns

Focus on basic logic - NOT like product companies!

MUST PREPARE

  • Arrays: Searching, Max/Min, Sorting
  • Strings: Palindrome, Reverse
  • Basic Math: Prime number, Fibonacci
  • Sorting Basics: Bubble sort, Selection sort
  • Patterns: Star patterns, Number patterns

NOT REQUIRED

  • Dynamic Programming
  • Advanced Graphs
  • Hard LeetCode Problems
  • Complex Data Structures
  • Tries & Segment Trees

IMPORTANT TIP:

Wipro focuses on basic coding. Practice arrays and strings thoroughly. Also prepare for essay writing - they may ask you to write on current topics.

Quick Tips for Success

Expert advice from successful candidates

Aptitude is Key

Focus on aptitude preparation - it's the main elimination round. Practice at least 100 questions from each topic.

Practice Arrays & Strings

Most Wipro coding questions are from arrays and strings. Practice at least 30-40 coding problems from these topics.

Prepare for Essay Writing

Wipro includes essay writing in the test. Prepare 2-3 current topics and practice writing a 250-word essay.

Know Your Projects

Be ready to explain your projects in detail. Have a clear understanding of technologies used and your role.

Time Management

Don't get stuck on difficult questions. Move on and come back if time permits. Speed matters in online tests.
