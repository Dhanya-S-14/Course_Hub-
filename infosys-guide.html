Infosys Springboard – Free Digital Learning Platform

Infosys Springboard is a free online learning platform by Infosys that offers courses in AI, data science, cloud, full‑stack development, life skills, and career‑readiness for students and professionals.

Through Springboard you can earn certificates and build industry‑relevant skills at no cost, making it an excellent resource for learners in India.

Learn more and access courses at: Infosys Springboard Official Page

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Visit Platform

Go to https://infyspringboard.onwingspan.com/

2

Register as Learner

Click "Register", select "Student", enter details and create account.

3

Verify & Complete Profile

Verify email, add college, skills, and interests.

4

Start Learning

Explore courses (AI, Programming, Soft Skills), click "Start Learning".

5

Apply for Women Program

Go to Programs / Announcements → Select "Pragati – Path to Future" → Click Apply Now.

6

Earn Certificates

Complete courses and get recognized certificates to boost your resume.

Infosys Springboard Pragati – Women‑Only Cohort Programs

Infosys Springboard runs a special women‑only program called "Pragati: Path to Future". This is a 3‑month / 100‑day, free, online upskilling journey designed exclusively for women (18+ years) in India, including students, graduates, job seekers, and career‑re-starters.

Each cohort (for example Cohort 7, 8, etc.) focuses on:

  • AI, digital skills, and tech‑aligned learning.
  • Career readiness, resume building, and personal branding.
  • Live sessions and mentorship from Infosys or industry experts.

Participants earn a certificate and gain exposure to Infosys' ecosystem, often with a hybrid model including virtual sessions and sometimes campus immersion.

To apply for the latest Pragati cohort, go to: Infosys Springboard Pragati Application Page
