Google DSA Patterns 🔥

Focus areas for Google interviews

MUST PREPARE (VERY IMPORTANT)

  • Dynamic Programming: Knapsack, LCS, Edit Distance, DP on Trees
  • Graphs: DFS, BFS, Dijkstra, Topological Sort
  • Trees: Binary Tree, BST, Tree Traversal
  • Backtracking: Subsets, Permutations, N-Queens
  • Bit Manipulation: XOR, Bit masking

REAL INSIGHT:

Google focuses on HARD problem-solving & optimization. They want to see optimal solutions with clean code.

Quick Tips for Success

Expert advice for Google interviews

Master Dynamic Programming

DP is the most important topic for Google. Practice at least 100 DP problems from LeetCode.

Graph Algorithms are Must

Be comfortable with BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, and graph traversals. Practice 50+ graph problems.

Solve 300-500 Problems

Focus on quality over quantity. Solve medium to hard problems from LeetCode Top 100.

Practice Problem Solving

Google values problem-solving approach. Explain your thinking before coding.

Know Time & Space Complexity

Always analyze time and space complexity. Optimize your solution.
