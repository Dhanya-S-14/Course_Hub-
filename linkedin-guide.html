LinkedIn Learning Enrollment Guide

Step-by-step guide to access free courses on LinkedIn Learning

LinkedIn Learning offers several free access options in 2026. The main methods include monthly unlocks, trials, promotional hubs, and library partnerships.

How to Get FREE Access

Monthly Free Unlocks

All LinkedIn members can unlock up to 10 courses per month for 24-hour access each!

  • Browse recommendations from homepage
  • Search for courses or view from profiles/feed
  • Click "Share this course" or "Unlock" on the right side
  • No trial needed!

Note: Courses auto-expire after 24 hours - plan binge-watching accordingly!

Free Trial (1 Month)

Sign up for a 1-month Premium trial to access the full 25,000+ course library.

  • Cancel before billing to avoid charges
  • Ideal for CS students targeting AI/Python courses during trial
  • Download certificates upon completion
1

Create a LinkedIn Account

  • Go to linkedin.com
  • Click Sign Up
  • Enter your email and create a password
  • Or sign up with Google/Apple
  • Complete your profile (optional but recommended)
2

Start Your Free Trial

  • Go to linkedin.com/learning
  • Click on any course to start
  • You'll be prompted to start 1-month free trial
  • Enter payment info (required for trial)
  • Cancel anytime before trial ends to avoid charges

Important

Set a reminder to cancel before the trial ends if you don't want to continue!

3

Explore Free Collections

LinkedIn offers various free course collections:

  • Promotional Hubs - Rotating free courses like "Time Management" or "Mindfulness"
  • Job Skills Collection - Free courses for career development
  • Productivity Collection - Time management and tools
  • Learning Paths - Structured curriculum
  • Check linkedin.com/learning/topics for all available course categories

Library & University Access

Free access through partnerships:

  • Public Libraries - Many libraries worldwide provide free LinkedIn Learning logins
  • Indian Libraries - Some state digital programs in Uttar Pradesh, Tamil Nadu offer free access
  • University Partnerships - Check your campus portal for free access
  • Viva Learning - Some organizations integrate free LinkedIn courses

For Students

Check your university/college portal for LinkedIn Learning partnerships - many institutions offer free access through campus licenses!

4

Start Learning

  • Search for courses by skill or topic
  • Watch video lessons at your pace
  • Download certificates upon completion
  • Add certificates to your LinkedIn profile
  • Track your learning progress

Quick Tips

  • Unlock up to 10 courses monthly for free (24-hour access each)
  • Start 1-month trial for full library access - cancel before billing
  • Check university portal for free campus access
  • Plan your learning - courses expire after 24 hours of unlocking
  • Download certificates to add to your LinkedIn profile
  • Use mobile app for learning on-the-go
  • Explore learning paths for structured curriculum
