TCS Selection Guide 2026

Complete preparation strategy for TCS NQT & Interviews

TCS Selection Rounds

📋 Round 1: TCS NQT (Online Test) - MOST IMPORTANT

  • Numerical Ability (Aptitude)
  • Verbal Ability
  • Reasoning Ability
  • Programming Logic
  • Coding (2 Questions)

⏱️ ~90+ Questions | ~180 Minutes

📋 Round 2: Technical Interview

  • Basic Coding Questions
  • DBMS & SQL
  • Operating Systems
  • OOP Concepts
  • Resume-based Questions

📋 Round 3: HR Interview

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Strengths & Weaknesses
  • Why TCS?
  • Bond Agreement Discussion

TCS DSA Patterns (Focus Areas)

TCS focuses on BASIC LOGIC - NOT like product companies!

✅ MUST PREPARE (Easy-Medium)

  • Arrays: Sum, Max/Min, Frequency count, Searching
  • Strings: Palindrome, Reverse, Count characters
  • Basic Math: Factorial, Prime number, Fibonacci
  • Sorting: Bubble sort, Selection sort
  • Recursion: Factorial, Fibonacci

❌ NOT REQUIRED (Mostly)

  • Dynamic Programming
  • Advanced Graphs
  • Hard LeetCode Problems
  • Complex Data Structures
💡 REAL INSIGHT from candidates:

"Focus on arrays and strings... basic coding is enough for TCS"

Quick Tips for Success

  • 1 Aptitude First: Focus 60% time on aptitude - it's the elimination round
  • 2 Basic DSA: Arrays & Strings are enough for coding round
  • 3 Communication: Speak clearly - very important for service companies
  • 4 Practice: Solve at least 50 aptitude + 30 coding questions
