About Skill India Mission

Skill India Mission is a Government of India initiative under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship that provides 100% free skill development courses with government-recognized certificates.

Eligibility Requirements

Who can apply?

  • Indian citizen
  • Minimum: 10th or 12th pass (varies by course)
  • Basic English or Hindi knowledge
  • Valid mobile number for OTP verification
  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory for registration)

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • Mobile Number (for OTP)
  • Email ID
  • Passport-size photo
  • Educational certificates

How to Enroll - Method 1: Online (Best)

Follow these simple steps to enroll in free courses online:

  1. Go to official portal: skillindiadigital.gov.in
  2. Click "Register / Sign Up"
  3. Enter your Name
  4. Enter Mobile Number (you'll receive OTP)
  5. Verify with OTP
  6. Enter Email and create Password
  7. Complete your profile (education, skills)
  8. Browse available courses
  9. Click "Enroll Now" on your chosen course
  10. Start learning instantly!

Entire process takes only a few minutes!

2

Method 2: Training Center (Offline)

Prefer in-person training? Here's how:

  • Visit Skill India website
  • Click "Find Training Center"
  • Enter your location/region
  • Choose a nearby training center
  • Visit the center with required documents
  • Register and attend physical training sessions

After Enrollment

Once enrolled, here's what to do:

  • Access courses in your "My Courses" dashboard
  • Watch video lessons
  • Complete quizzes and assessments
  • Track your progress
  • Download certificate after completing the course
